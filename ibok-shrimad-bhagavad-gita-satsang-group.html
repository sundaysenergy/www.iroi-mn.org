GEOKC offers a great devotional program called Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group to join its activities. This program was created by Pujya Guruji to spread the knowledge of holy Gita through Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group in our society on national and international levels. 

By spreading the basic non-sectarian universal teachings of Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita and other Vedic scriptures, GEOKC is very thankful to all community members who spent time with us to join the activities of Gita satsang. Through this devotional program and related activities, all community members learn and help us to create a faithful religious community. This program gives us Vedic knowledge of Hindu scripture as well as the brilliant idea to promote the universal brotherhood of humankind.

Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group provides support and guidance to devotees to establish a true relationship with them, not only for a certain limit of time but for always as our friends, which also signifies that they are true devotees of God.

This program is specially created to make a faith-based community and offers many useful services and benefits to both our society and all types of people. We distribute holy gita to help and support people in many areas including educational institutions, temples, libraries, prisons, courts, and other public places.

About Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita

The beautiful collection of heavenly messages of Lord Krishna

The Bhagavad-Gita or Song of God is a Sanskrit text from the chapter Bhishma Parva of the Mahabharata epic, comprising 700 verses and 18 chapters. The spiritual discourse on Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita is divided into several parts. All parts are created and based on the literary knowledge of Bhagavad-Gita describing the significance and meaning of the text. These parts include:

  • Introduction and background of gita
  • One of the most important Hindu Scripture
  • Date and Text (Mahabharata)
  • Kurukshetra war and Lord Krishna
  • Lord Krishna as the speaker
  • Lord Krishna as the Supreme Being Himself (Svayam Bhagavan)
  • Overview of 18 chapters
  • Scripture of yoga:
    • Eighteen yogas
    • Bhakti yoga
    • Message of Bhagavad-Gita
  • Influence of Bhagavad-Gita:
    • Holy Bhagavad-Gita (a holy text, other known as Bible or Koran)
    • The authority of gita (used as holy text in many areas including: educational institutions, temples, libraries, prisons, courts, and other public places)
    • Commentators of GEOKC
  • Bhagavad-Gita is one of the world’s great spiritual texts (increase spirituality)

Main Steps

Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group is working all around the world and provides many devotional services which give us a deep knowledge to understand all aspects of our human life with a sense of humanity and spirituality. In Bhagavad-Gita, Lord Krishna talks to Arjuna on inner peace and happiness. If you are interested in Bhagavad Gita or spirituality and really want to understand Bhakti Yoga in general, you can join this devotional program and get many benefits.

Anyone is welcome to join the Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group and take parts in the activities on the regular basis. Altogether there are ten steps including:

  1. Group meditation, singing devotional bhajans and kirtan.
  2. Distribution of Bhagavad Gita to all devotees, spiritual discourses on Gita given by the GEOKC main head guru (Pujya Guruji) of the program of GEOKC Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group, and chapters overviews (summary and discourse on 18 chapters including 700 verses one by one).
  3. Participation and group discussion of each chapter begin with the first chapter by Pujya Guruji of the program of GEOKC Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group, including a detailed explanation of the chapter and its verses to present the meaning and interpretation of the verses during the Satsang.
  4. The spiritual master always provides support and guidance for self study and advice on practicing at home for at lease 30 minutes daily. He also gives advice to everyone not to leave until all chapters are completed.
  5. Sessions begin on the first Friday of the month of January to continue the Gita Satsang, until the last Friday of the month of December, each year including all activities of the Gita Satsang Group, which provides great knowledge and experience to everyone. Every Friday a new session begins and continues until all chapters are completed, including discourses and discussion.
  6. After the end of the discussion of any chapter (every Friday), there will be an offering, a prayer for blessing of the deity (Lord Krishna), Gita Aarti, and prasad distribution.
  7. Every Friday sponsor family or any sponsor with the help of volunteers prepare fresh prasad in Lord Krishna's kitchen for all devotees. First they offered prasad to deity (Lord Krishna) and then they distribute to devotees.
  8. For offering purpose on every Friday, everyone is welcome to bring fruits, nuts, flowers, and incense to offer to Lord Krishna (deity).
  9. The devotional tour ends on each Friday with the blessing of Guru and Prasad distribution.
  10. Participation and group discussion of all chapters is a non-stop process for spiritual progress of all devotees of GEOKC. To join, please check the Program Schedule (below).
