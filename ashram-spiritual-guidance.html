Ashram Spiritual Guidance

(To develop faith, friendship, love and devotion)
An Ashram conducted by a true Guru serving but God to bring
Hindu community towards a glorious and divine realization

By the Grace of God we are happily and successfully surviving and trying to touch the common ground of faith, friendship, love, and devotion. Some bhakti related programs are specially created to touch this ground as a prominent sign of bhakti or devotion. These programs are really great specifically for new participants and for those who are not familiar with the Ashram yet. Ashram is also a good source to bring all Hindu community members together and join its devotional programs to gain knowledge.
