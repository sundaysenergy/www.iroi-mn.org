Ways to Give

IBOK Way to Give includes the different names of campaigns or ways to give which fulfill our mission and also the required needs of fundraising to keep it active. To build a Hindu community internationally IBOK invites all Hindu members, living anywhere on this earth to be a part to join our activities to benefit themselves via supporting some Seva as bhakti. Way to Give describes the main reason of our current needs also the needs for future. It’s give the explanation of lifelong financial stability with the correct definition of our funding needs to keep IBOK mission active to be followed by next generation.

The names of these campaigns are now related with Annual Fund, Major Gift & Planned Giving, Capital Campaigns, Bhoomi Dann, Endowment Fund, Tribute Gift, Donor Recognition Wall, and Other Giving Sevas of IBOK. Bhoomi Daan
The Bhoomi Daan of IBOK is a project which include all selected international branches of IBOK to proceed by geographical location including all different locations one by one to request Seva (donation). IBOK of MN is the first branch to start the project. And to complete this project IBOK Campaigns team members spreading all around the world including:

Tribute Gifts
A Tribute Gift is a kind of gift, payment, declaration, or other acknowledgment of gratitude, respect, or admiration to a family member, friend or to a dearest one to remember as loved one. Tribute gifts can also describe a sum of money or a contribution of praise paid to another.
IBOK provides a great fundraising option to all of our donors, participants or anyone to contribute to those who are most loving to them to remember. It’s a great fundraising option which is just related to honor someone as charity.
