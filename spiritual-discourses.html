All spiritual discourses related programs are very educational and created to increase spirituality. Actually this is the first step of any Devotee who join IBOK to listen the voice of spiritual master (Pujya Guruji). These programs are required to join by all devotees or itâ€™s a recommended practice to understand all related subjects properly. After gaining the knowledge of all these subjects any participant can easily follow all other types of programs. The complete information about these spiritual discourse related programs including how to join, learn, practices, and benefits are also included in each of the following programs:

  • Program of Religion or Belief System (spiritual discourse, workshop, slide show, and presentation)
  • Program of Hindu and Hinduism (spiritual discourse, workshop, slide show, presentations, and books distribution)
  • Program of Hindu Belief and Practices (spiritual discourse, workshop, slide shows, and presentations)
  • Program of Bhakti and Bhakti Yoga (Belief in Lord Krishna, initiation, Guru Sishya Parampara, Satsang Japa, Mantra, Sadhana, Dhayan, Meditation, Samadhi, workshops, slide shows, and presentations)
  • Program of Hindu Scriptures (spiritual discourse, workshop, slide shows, presentations, and books distribution)
  • Program of Importance of Satsang (Satsang means (1) the company of the highest truth, (2) the company of a guru (spiritual master), and (3) company with an assembly of persons who listen to, talk about, and assimilate the truth including: spiritual discourse, workshop, and slide shows, and presentations)
  • Program of Importance of Ashram and Gurus (based on the importance of ashram and gurus in our life and related with the foundation of Gopinath Bhakti Ashram, its gurus and its Seekers Spiritual Programs including: spiritual discourse, workshop, IBOK ashramâ€™s campaign goal, slide shows, and presentations)
