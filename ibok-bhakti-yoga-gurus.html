IBOK Bhakti Yoga Gurus Project

(IBOK Gurus Project is a gurus inheritance project)

Dedication of Gurus to Lila Avatar Lord Shri Krishna

The journey of these Bhakti Yoga Gurus begins from Mathura and Vrindavan (India) to come to the USA, and then all around the world, for the purpose of providing a specific path of Bhakti to all of our participants.

Minnesota as a First Branch of IBOK: After the expiration of the first Main Head Guru (Pujya Guruji) his teaching will automatically will be inherit to next new Main Head Guru (Pujya Guruji) of MN branch which will be selected only by first Main Head Guru (Pujya Guruji). This process will be constantly on for the purpose of next generation to next generation.

Other Gurus will work only under the guidance of first Main Head Guru (Pujya Guruji) to call by their titles of IBOK Head Gurus, and IBOK Gurus. After the expiration of these IBOK Head Gurus and IBOK Gurus, their teaching will automatically will be inherited to next new IBOK Head Gurus and IBOK Gurus which will be selected only by IBOK Head Gurus and IBOK Gurus to call with the same titles of IBOK Head Gurus and IBOK Gurus. This process will be constantly on for the purpose of next generation to next generation.

All Other International Branches of IBOK: Other international branches of IBOK will follow the same process as the first branch of IBOK which is established in Minnesota for the purpose of to keep IBOK active all around the world with the memory of these IBOK Bhakti Yoga Gurus.
