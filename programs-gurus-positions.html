Programs' Gurus Positions

Position: IBOK Main Head Guru (Pujya Guruji)

Program/service: Satsang Services

Qualifications: Degree and education in Hinduism or related Hindu religious education is required. Other qualification such as knowledge of Hindu scriptures, Hindu beliefs and practices, all types of yoga specially the deep knowledge of bhakti yoga, initiation, meditation (to give instructions to all devotees), spiritual discourses, recitations, devotional music, worship, and prayer are must to be qualified for this position. This spiritual master must also have knowledge and devotion about Satvic Food (without onion and garlic) to explain to all participants to join after Satsang. One of the great responsibilities of this spiritual master is to be very active and supportive in all areas of IBOK Vaishnava Events (observances) to help IBOK as IBOK is a Vaishnava Bhakti Movement.

Position: IBOK Main Head Guru (Pujya Guruji)

Program/Service: IBOK Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita Satsang Group

Required Qualifications: Knowledge of Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita, Hindu scriptures, knowledge of spiritual discourses, writing and editing ability, knowledge and devotion about Satvic Food (without onion and garlic) to explain to all participants to join after Gita Satsang and other qualities to complete all tasks kindly including: support and guidance to all participants who are joining this program, Gita distribution, program related presentations and slide shows, and plan for devotee’s future goal.

Position: IBOK Head Guru #4

Program/Service: Spiritual Discourses

Similar Qualifications: We are looking similar qualifications for the position of Spiritual Discourse-Related Programs to apply. Degree and education in Hinduism or related Hindu religious education is required. Other qualification such as knowledge Hindu scriptures, Hindu beliefs and practices, Vaishnavism (one of the largest branch of Hinduism), all types of yoga specially the deep knowledge of bhakti yoga, meditation, spiritual discourses, devotional music, worship, and prayer are must to be qualified for this position.

Knowledge and devotion about Satvic Food (without onion and garlic) to explain to all participants to join after Satsang.

Position: IBOK Head Guru #1

Special Attraction: Initiation with IBOK Main Head Guruji

Program/Service: Bhakti Sadhana

For: Services of Yoga Center (Practicing Rules & Methods)

Required Qualifications: Must have knowledge of Hinduism, all types of Yoga (for special instruction on Bhakti Yoga and Meditation), Vaishnavism, Sadhana, Samadhi, spiritual discourses, and Vedic knowledge. Must be knowledgeable about IBOK Bhakti Yoga Meditation. Able to control and handle all instruction related with this service in front of all audience as it is an instructional service. Serve with all Hindu community people on permanent basis for the purpose of to keep them in touch with IBOK constantly.

Knowledge and devotion about Satvic Food (without onion and garlic) to explain to all participants to join after they done with this activity as it’s a part of this activity.

Position: IBOK Head Guru #6

Program/Service: Program of Ten Avatars of Lord Vishnu

Required Qualifications: Must have a degree or religious education in Hinduism including all parts of Hinduism (Hindu beliefs and practices, Hindu scripture, Bhakti Yoga, Vaishnavism and more). Knowledge related with the study of Lord Vishnu Scriptures and His Avatars are must to be qualified for this position. Yearly observances of IBOK Vaishnava Events is a special reminder for this position which we follow on yearly basis to remind all audience who are joining this service to join the IBOK Vaishnava Events regularly.

Able to handle presentations, slide shows, IBOK Traditional Worship Ceremony and other required tasks related with this program.

Knowledge and devotion about Satvic Food (without onion and garlic) to explain to all participants to join after this service as nourishment.

Position: IBOK Guru #1

Position: IBOK Head Guru #7

Programs/Services: We are looking for some Spiritual Masters for our three devotional music programs, which are as follows:

  • IBOK Shri Krishna Murari Bhakti Movement (devotional chanting using traditional musical instruments used in Kirtan)
  • IBOK Traditional Bhajans (devotional singing using traditional musical instruments used in Bhajans)
  • IBOK Kirtan Heritage (devotional chanting using traditional musical instruments used in Kirtan)

Required Qualifications: Degree from an accredited unversity or college in Hindu religion (Hinduism, Hindu scripture, Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, Devotional Music), knowledge of traditional instruments used in devotional music (bhajans, kirtan, dhun, amritvani, etc.), knowledge of hymns, Vedic mantras or mantras, strotra, chanting, composing, singing, and an ability to perform Kirtan in public (the most existing modes of Kirtan) are the rquired qualifications to be consider for this position. The qualified canditate must have 5 to 10 yeras devotional music experience to fulfill the need of this position.

The IBOK Head Guru #7 and IBOK Guru #1 both will be in charge of these devotional musical programs, and work under the guidance of IBOK Main Head Guru.
