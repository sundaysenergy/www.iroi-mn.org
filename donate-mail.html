Donate by Mail

IBOK welcomes all those donors who really want to donate by mail using their checks or money order to support the IBOK Annual Fund. To process your donation please first print out the following mail-in donation form and then complete all information included in this form. After the completion of this form you can mail it to our mailing address which you can found inside in mail-in donation form.

IBOK is a 501(c)(3) charitable non-profit organization, under the regulations of the Internal Revenue Service.  All contributions to this organization are tax-deductible to the extent provided by law.

All donors or individuals must be 18 years of age or older to donate.
