IBOK make full effort to obtain gifts on a yearly basis to support mostly general operations budget regularly and thankful to those donors who are willing to participate and want to be permanent donors or devotees of IBOK.

IBOK Annual Fund and the process to achieve the goal and mission of IBOK is a part of donation or Seva and it is naturally build to follow to reach to the last step, while continue supporting the spiritual, religious, educational, social and cultural needs of IBOK. This really help the General Operation Budget and support the devotional developmental programs of IBOK to conduct into the multiple facilities locations including IBOK Bhawan, Ashram Bhawan, and Temple.

What is an IBOK Annual Giving Donation?

The constant needs of IBOK, program impact areas, and the spiritual, religious and educational needs of IBOK is making an annual giving donation.

IBOK Annual Giving is the best opportunity to support IBOK and keep it active to benefit Hindu community with the opportunity to be a permanent part of IBOK. Other community members can also get chance to join IBOK or its activities with the same quantity of respect. All donors have equal chance to select any amount or inter by their own choice to give to IBOK Annual Fund to help and support all causes of Annual Giving via receiving their full benefits.

There are several different ways or options to make donation to our non-profit organization. These options are as follows:

  • Donate Online (using major credit cards)
  • Donate by Mail (using check or money order

You can select any option to donate either online OR by mail of this page. All important information are already included in both online and mail-in application forms to complete the application process properly.
